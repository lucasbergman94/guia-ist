🧭 The relocation roadmap›

Phase 1 — Documents

  • Apostille diploma, transcripts, teaching cert and a 2-years-experience letter (each separately).
  • Email color scans to HR — no Turkish translation needed.

Phase 2 — Ministry pre-approval (Ön İzin)

  • The Ministry pre-approves your assignment before the work permit. HR submits; it's the slowest part.

Phase 3 — Work visa

  • Apply online; book at the Turkish mission where you reside or hold citizenship; HR checks your form first.

Phase 4 — Flights & arrival

  • Book your own flight (economy) into IST only after approval. Pets: start the rabies titer test early.

Phase 5 — First weeks

  • Turkish SIM (starts the 120-day IMEI clock), tax number, İstanbulkart, e-Devlet, bank, address registration.
📄 Documents & apostille›
  • Apostille (each separately): diploma, transcripts, teaching certificate, experience letter, police certificate if asked.
  • Experience letter: from your previous school, on letterhead, signed.
  • US: via the issuing state's Secretary of State or the US Dept. of State (cheap).
  • Non-Hague country: Ministry of Foreign Affairs, then the nearest Turkish mission.
  • Email color scans to HR; no physical originals; no Turkish translation (the school does it); use a hard-copy apostille, not digital.
  • A simple doctor's "fit to work" note may also be requested.

🏠 Housing & commute›

Open the interactive map →

  • Walking-distance: İstinye, Maslak, Emirgan (Sarıyer). The bus runs from 9 stops.
  • sahibinden.com/en · hepsiemlak.com/en. Sahibinden needs a Turkish number to message agents — use "call" then WhatsApp.
  • Furnished is scarce; don't pay a year upfront for an unseen place; many use an Airbnb first and view in person.
🧰 Arrival setup›
  • Tax number (vergi numarası): free, online.
  • e-Devlet: get the password at a PTT.
  • Bank: passport, tax number, address, contract.
  • SIM: register your IMEI within 120 days or buy a local phone.
  • İstanbulkart + address registration (adres kayıt).
🌆 Daily life in Istanbul›
  • Transport: metro, metrobüs, ferries (İstanbulkart) + the school bus.
  • Apps: Moovit, BiTaksi, Yemeksepeti/Getir, Google Translate (offline Turkish).
  • Pets: rabies titer test to enter. Sport: ENKA teacher discount. Emergency: 112.

❓ FAQ›
  • Visa wait normal? Yes, often weeks; the Ministry controls it. The school tracks it.
  • Translate documents? No — the school does it. You only apostille.
  • Book flights when? After approval. Into IST.
  • Furnished? Rare; plan to furnish (IKEA run available).
